Small Business IT Checklist

Home Tips Contact

If you're starting or running a small business, your IT setup can make or break your daily operations. Here's a simple checklist to stay protected, productive, and professional:

1. Business Email & Domain

  • Use a custom email (e.g., info@yourbusiness.com) via Google Workspace or Microsoft 365.
  • Avoid using Gmail, Yahoo, or Hotmail for business communication.

2. Secure Backup System

  • Use Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox to store files in the cloud.
  • Back up local files weekly to a physical hard drive or NAS.

3. Antivirus & Firewall

  • Install reliable antivirus software (Bitdefender, Norton, etc.) on all business devices.
  • Use built-in firewall settings or network routers with firewall protection.

4. Device Management

  • Label business devices, set passwords, and enable full disk encryption (e.g. BitLocker or FileVault).

5. Remote IT Support

  • Have a trusted IT support provider (like Nedson) ready to assist with updates, issues, or user training.
  • Consider remote monitoring for proactive maintenance.
