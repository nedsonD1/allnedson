About GroupN

GroupN is not a single company but an umbrella identity representing a family of registered businesses owned and managed together. It brings structure and identity to the work we do across industries, locations, and sectors.

Our Purpose

GroupN was born from the desire to simplify and unify different ventures in Information Technology, Real Estate, Finance, and Charity. Though these ventures operate independently, GroupN connects them with a shared vision of innovation, excellence, and impact.

Our Brands

  • Nedson Innovations Ltd (UK): IT Consultancy and Property Letting in the UK.
  • Nedson Technologies (Nigeria): Tech solutions, training, and hardware sales in Nigeria.
  • Nedson Properties & Finance Ltd (Nigeria): Real estate investments and financial services.
  • TenderCare NG: Non-profit providing educational, nutritional, and empowerment support.

Our Journey

  • 2020: Started with IT services in the UK
  • 2021: Expanded into real estate letting and investment
  • 2022: Opened Nigerian tech and charity arms
  • 2024: Launched finance & ROI investment program
