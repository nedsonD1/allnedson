How to Fix a Slow Computer

If your computer is slow, it doesn’t always mean you need to replace it. Try these quick steps to boost speed and performance:

1. Restart Your Computer

Restarting clears memory and stops unnecessary background apps.

2. Disable Startup Apps

  • Windows: Ctrl + Shift + Esc → Startup tab → Right-click to disable
  • Mac: System Settings → Users & Groups → Login Items

3. Uninstall Unused Apps

Remove programs you haven’t used in months to free resources.

4. Clear Storage Space

Delete large files, empty Recycle Bin, and use disk cleanup tools.

5. Update Software

Update Windows/macOS, antivirus, and device drivers regularly.
